Beijing KSM Technologies

Multi-year design collaboration spanning corporate identity, product R&D, and a patented sub-brand

Overview

A multi-year design collaboration with Beijing KSM Technologies — a technology manufacturer specialising in water treatment and environmental systems. The engagement evolved from an initial placement-year visual identity refresh into an ongoing relationship covering corporate identity system development, product R&D support, and full product design for a new sub-brand.

The work spans five years across multiple phases: CI/VI system design, enclosure and structural R&D, and the creation of the NX Water sub-brand including brand identity, product design, and a Chinese design patent.

Phase 1: Visual Identity Refresh

Lead contributor

The initial engagement during a placement year (2020) delivered a comprehensive visual identity refresh for KSM Technologies. Starting with a brand audit across print, digital, and environmental applications, the work established a cohesive CI/VI system adopted across the company's internal and external communications.

The identity system covered logo refinement, typography, colour systems, stationery, digital templates, and environmental signage — creating a unified brand language that the company continues to use across all touchpoints.

CI/VI system overview — logo, typography, colour
Brand applications — stationery, digital, environmental

Phase 2: Product R&D

Lead contributor

Following the identity work, the engagement expanded into product R&D support — collaborating with KSM's internal engineering team on enclosure design, thermal management, and airflow optimisation for product housings. This phase focused on resolving practical engineering constraints while maintaining design coherence across the product range.

Design consultation covered form development, CMF (colour, material, finish) specification, and manufacturing feasibility assessment. R&D collaboration addressed material selection and prototype review for new hardware categories being brought to market.

Product enclosure design / thermal analysis
CMF development / material samples

Phase 3: NX Water Sub-brand

Lead contributor

Led the complete product design for KSM's NX Water sub-brand — a line of water treatment products targeting the consumer and commercial market. The work covered the full scope: brand identity creation (logo and typeface), filter housing design, control panel interfaces, and exterior aesthetics across the product range.

The filter housing design, which balanced manufacturing constraints with user serviceability and visual coherence, was granted a Chinese design patent in December 2025 — the first IP outcome from the collaboration.

Reflection

The KSM engagement demonstrates what a long-term design relationship can produce when there's genuine integration between design and engineering teams. What started as a placement-year identity project evolved into ongoing R&D collaboration and eventually a full sub-brand launch with IP outcomes.

The most valuable aspect was working across the full spectrum — from brand strategy and visual systems through to thermal analysis and manufacturing tolerances — within a single client relationship. Each phase informed the next: understanding the brand language made product design coherent, and understanding manufacturing constraints made the brand applications realistic.
