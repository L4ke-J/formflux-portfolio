Other Work

Smaller studies, experiments, and side projects.

vasé — Urban Farming Tools

RSA Student Design Awards brief: design a product, system, or service using sustainable food and farming to improve health or well-being.

Inspired by bonsai cultivation and ikebana (Japanese flower arrangement), vasé is a set of three gardening tools — trowelling, shearing, and watering — designed for small-scale indoor growing. The organic forms reference natural growth patterns while the material palette prioritises recyclability. The project explored how ritual and aesthetics can encourage urban residents to engage with food growing as a daily practice.

RSA Brief Product Concept Sustainability

Cooktop Concept: Heat Hatch

Compact, foldable cooktop for city apartments with tiny kitchens.

Designed during a placement at Design 2 Gather (Shanghai), Heat Hatch uses conduction heating with integrated smoke extraction and task lighting. The folding mechanism lets the unit collapse flat when not in use, reclaiming counter space. The concept addressed a real market gap for users who want to cook properly but are constrained by micro-kitchen layouts common in urban China and the UK.

Consumer Product Small-Kitchen Design

Blender Station: Blend Box

Integrated blender station expanding the functionality of the hand-held blender.

Also developed at Design 2 Gather, Blend Box integrates tools and functions into a countertop blender station. The concept was born from observing users who blend while cooking — needing quick access, easy cleanup, and noise containment. The station consolidates attachments, provides a dedicated prep surface, and encloses the blender during operation to reduce noise in open-plan living spaces.

About

I'm a product and systems designer who turns research into working prototypes. I combine computational design, material testing, and additive manufacturing to build and iterate under real constraints, and to improve the workflows that make projects deliverable.

Recent work includes leading robotic extrusion workflows across multiple platforms and reducing setup inefficiencies by ~40% across testing cycles, as well as collaborating with R&D teams to resolve practical engineering issues such as heat, airflow, stability, and usability in product enclosures. I'm at my best when translating complex constraints into clear, testable design decisions and shipping outcomes.
